Carnage

Who is Carnage?

Carnage is a blood-red alien symbiote that bonds with Cletus Kasady, creating one of the most dangerous villains in the Marvel Universe. Unlike Venom, Carnage is purely sadistic and psychopathic, lacking any moral compass. His chaotic nature makes him an unpredictable and nearly unstoppable force.

Abilities & Powers

Carnage possesses enhanced strength, speed, and reflexes, surpassing both Spider-Man and Venom. He can regenerate from almost any injury, form weapons with his symbiote, and even plant parts of himself into others, infecting them with his symbiote offspring.
